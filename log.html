BUS PASS MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Eligibility

To be eligible for a bus pass, applicants must provide valid personal information, including name, address, and ID proof (Aadhar number). Students must provide a valid student ID card.

2. Pass Validity

- Daily Pass: Valid for 24 hours from the time of issue.
- Monthly Pass: Valid for 30 days from the issue date.
- Yearly Pass: Valid for 1 year from the issue date.

3. Fees and Payments

All pass fees must be paid in advance. Payment once made is non-refundable. A static payment ID will be provided during the registration process.

4. Use of Bus Pass

The pass is non-transferable and must be carried during travel. Passholders are required to show the pass upon request by the bus conductor or authorities.

5. Penalties

Misuse of the bus pass, including sharing or tampering, will result in a penalty and confiscation of the pass. Legal action may also be taken in case of fraud.

6. Lost or Damaged Pass

If the bus pass is lost or damaged, passholders must immediately report it. A duplicate pass may be issued after payment of a replacement fee.

7. Terms Acceptance

By registering for a bus pass, applicants agree to abide by these terms and conditions. The management reserves the right to change the terms at any time.
