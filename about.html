About the System

The Bus Pass Management System is a web-based platform designed to simplify the process of applying for and managing bus passes. It caters to the needs of regular commuters by making the application process quick, transparent, and hassle-free.

Who Can Apply

  • Students commuting to schools or colleges.
  • Employees traveling to workplaces.
  • Senior citizens eligible for special concessions.
  • Daily commuters using public transportation regularly.

Special Features

  1. Easy online registration and renewal.
  2. Multiple pass types: daily, monthly, and quarterly.
  3. Secure payment options with a unique payment ID.
  4. Real-time updates on bus routes and timings.
  5. Environment-friendly digital passes.
